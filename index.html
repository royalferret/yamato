Our Story

A warm welcome
always awaits

We are delighted to welcome back our existing friends as well as new ones to Mikado's in Cherry Hill. We know you'll enjoy your time with us — we want to make sure your experience is always exceptional.

Please enjoy our sizzling hibachi steak, served tableside, and our amazing sushi offerings, all totally prepared on site. Since we are a BYOB, we invite you to bring your favorite bottle to make your dining experience even nicer!

Besides our many, very spacious inside dining areas, we also offer comfortable outside dining as well as 2 party rooms seating up to 10 guests per room for your special events. Let our hosts Kenny & Tony Lin and our entire staff welcome you with a smile.
