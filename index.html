Our Story

A warm welcome
always awaits

We are excited to welcome both our returning guests and new visitors to Yamato in Marlton. Our goal is to make every visit memorable, and we look forward to serving you a truly enjoyable dining experience.

Enjoy our fresh hibachi-grilled entrées cooked right in front of you, along with a wide selection of handcrafted sushi rolls, all prepared fresh daily. As a BYOB restaurant, we encourage you to bring your favorite beverages to complement your meal.

In addition to our spacious and comfortable indoor seating, we also offer outdoor dining and private party rooms that accommodate up to 12 guests each, perfect for celebrations and gatherings. Our team looks forward to welcoming you with warm hospitality.
